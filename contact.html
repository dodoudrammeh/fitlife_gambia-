Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common Questions

Do I need prior experience?

No! FitLife Gambia welcomes members of all fitness levels. Our trainers will help you get started.

Can I cancel my membership?

Yes, you can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. No hidden fees or long contracts required.

What is included in membership?

Membership includes gym access, locker rooms, and showers. Premium plans include classes and personal training.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes! Our certified trainers offer personalized sessions at competitive rates. Ask our staff for details.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have ample free parking for our members. Secure parking is available upon request.
